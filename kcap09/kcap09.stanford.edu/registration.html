Included in the Conference Fees:

Along with a copy of the proceedings of the main conference, the fees include:

  • A poster reception, which will take place in the evening of Wednesday September 2nd
  • A ticket for the conference banquet, which will take place in the evening of Thursday September 3rd

If you have any dietary needs, allergies or other requirements you can either tell us on the relevant page of the registration procedure, or you should be able to log in to RegOnline and change your details; however to ensure we are aware of the changes, you may also wish to fill in a special form that will be available shortly.

Payment Methods

You will be required to pay your registration fees when you register using the RegOnline site, which will be available shortly. Accepted methods of payment are Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Diners Club and American Express credit cards. These will also be accepted for on-site registration. It is also possible to pay via wire/bank transfer (this option will not be available on-site): to do this, select the "Wire Transfer" payment option, complete registration and contact us for details about making the money transfer.

Student Assistants

We need several students to help us organize the conference, workshops and tutorials; duties will involve helping with registration, with ensuring sessions have appropriate audio-visual equipment, and that the K-CAP 2009 wireless network is functioning, and to generally support the organizing committee. In return for these activities, you will get a fixed amount of funds as a reimbursment for expenses related to travel and conference (registration, flight, hotel).

Application procedure: you should send email to the conference organizers by July 15 outlining your interests and background relevant to the conference, and an outline of your expected total cost to attend K-CAP 2009. You should also provide the name of your supervisor, your thesis topic and your department. By the same deadline you should ask your supervisor to email us, with a statement confirming he / she is your supervisor, and confirming you are a registered research student.

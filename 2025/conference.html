Venue and Accomodations

📍 Venue

K-CAP 2025 will be hosted at Wright State University, located in Dayton, Ohio, USA. The university campus offers a modern, well-equipped environment ideal for networking, presentations, and immersive sessions at the intersection of knowledge representation, AI, and intelligent systems.

Getting Here

We recommend Dayton International Airport (DAY) as the most convenient arrival point. However, two additional airports are accessible for domestic and international attendees.

✈️ Dayton International Airport (DAY)– Recommended

  • Cost estimate: ~$20–30 USD

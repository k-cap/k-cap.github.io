K-CAP Governance:
Upcoming Elections and New Steering Committee

To participate in the elections and to receive general information about the K-CAP conference, please subscribe to the K-CAP mailing list

September 13, 2013

SIGAI accepted a governance proposal submitted jointly by the chairs of recent K-CAP conferences. The proposal includes a provision to jumpstart the process with a Founding Steering Committee, whose members are the chairs of the last four K-CAP conferences. The Founding Steering Committee will advertise to SIGAI members and to the broader K-CAP community an announcement for elections for a Steering Committee.

June 13, 2013

ACM SIGAI is the sponsor of the K-CAP conference series, and encourages conferences to be managed with a democratic and stable governance structure. The organizers of the most recent K-CAP conference series initiated a discussion to formalize the governance structure of the conference.

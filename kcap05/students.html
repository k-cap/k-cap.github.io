Student Grants

A limited number of grants are available towards the cost of registration, travel and accomodation for students who are currently enrolled in a PhD program. Those receiving grants are expected to help out with light duties at the conference, such as registration and audio-visual setup. To apply, please send email to kremer@cpsc.ucalgary.ca and blythe@isi.edu with your name, your host institution, your advisor's name and email address, your papers at the conference if any, and an estimate of your travel costs for the conference.

Included in the Conference Fees:

Along with a copy of the proceedings of the main conference, the fees include:

  • A poster reception, which will take place in the evening of Monday, June 27th.
  • A ticket for the conference banquet, which will take place in the evening of Tuesday June 28th
  • Access to any of the three workshops and two tutorials co-located with the conference

If you have any dietary needs, allergies or other requirements you can either tell us on the relevant page of the registration procedure, or you should be able to log in to RegOnline and change your details.

Other meals (e.g., lunches or breakfasts) will not be provided unless you are registered in the conference hotel, which we strongly encourage since the hotel is rather far from the Banff city centre and there are not many amenities around.

Payment Methods

You will be required to pay your registration fees when you register using the RegOnline site. Accepted methods of payment are Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Diners Club and American Express credit cards. These will also be accepted for on-site registration. It is also possible to pay via wire/bank transfer (this option will not be available on-site): to do this, select the "Wire Transfer" payment option, complete registration and contact us for details about making the money transfer.

Cancellation policy

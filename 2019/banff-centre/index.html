Conference venue

K-CAP 2019 will take place on the 19-21 November 2019 in Marina del Rey, California, which is a scenic fishing village located in Los Angeles County.

The conference will be hosted at the Marina del Rey Marriott, a major hotel that is just 4-miles north from Los Angeles International Airport (LAX), 3-blocks from the beautiful sandy beaches of Venice Beach and 2-miles south from Santa Monica shopping attractions. The hotel offers easy access to a variety of Los Angeles area highlights, in addition to providing access to over 30 restaurants within walking distance.

Booking

We have reserved a small number of rooms at the Marriott for conference attendees between the nights of 15-24 November at a rate of 199 USD per night. These rooms may be reserved on a first-come-first-serve basis till there is availability or before Tuesday, October 29, whichever comes earlier. Please use this link to book and direct any inquiries to kejriwal@isi.edu

Additionally, Marina del Rey (and more broadly LA County) has many accommodation options, including inns, hotels and short-term rentals like Airbnb for travelers of all budgets.

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)

Marina del Rey, like much of Los Angeles County, is easily accessible via flight, rail and car. For those flying into Los Angeles County, LAX is the most convenient airport, although the county also has a number of smaller regional airports, including including Hollywood Burbank Airport, John Wayne Airport, Long Beach Airport, and Ontario International Airport. LAX services daily international and domestic flights to most other major (and many non-major) cities, and is only a 15 minute drive from the conference venue.
